Quetion 4:-What are HTML Entities?

Ans:-Entities are frequently used to display special characters or symbols and invisible characters (like non-breaking spaces).

Entities:

  • <  :-less than
  • >  :-greater than
  • & :-ampersand
  • "  :-double quotation mark
  • '   :-single quotation mark
  •     :-non-breaking space
