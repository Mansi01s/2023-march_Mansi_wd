Quetion 20:-What are logical and physical tags in HTML?

Ans:-
 Logical tag:- When we enclose the text in the strong tag, it tells the browser that enclosed text is more important than other texts.
  Example:-<strong>
 
  Physical tag:- Physical tags are used to tell the browser how to display the text enclosed in the physical tag.
  Example:- <b>,  <big>,  <i>
